Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 15, 2024

Introduction

Course Caddy LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Course Caddy mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the application includes:

  • Personal Data: Information you provide when creating an account, such as your name and email address.
  • Location Data: We may request access to your device's location to provide weather-based features for golf courses.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the application, including your interactions with features.

Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our application
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Provide weather-based calculations and recommendations
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions

Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To service providers who assist us in operating our application (e.g., payment processors, weather data providers)

Third-Party Services

Our application may integrate with third-party services to provide weather data and other features. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
