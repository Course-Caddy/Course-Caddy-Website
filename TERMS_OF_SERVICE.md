## Description of Service

Course Caddy is a mobile application developed by Course Caddy LLC that helps golfers calculate adjusted club distances based on environmental conditions including temperature, elevation, humidity, wind, and lie conditions.

### Free Features:
- Golf bag management with club distances
- Environmental condition adjustments (manual entry)
- Baseline variable configuration
- Distance calculations based on temperature, elevation, and humidity
- Lie conditions calculator
- Putt break calculator
- Ball flight characteristics calculator

### Pro Features (Subscription Required):
- Auto-fill weather from GPS or city search
- Wind calculator with 8-direction support
- USGA-compliant yardage card PDF generation
- Multiple golf bag storage (up to 5 bags)
- Real-time weather updates
- Priority support
