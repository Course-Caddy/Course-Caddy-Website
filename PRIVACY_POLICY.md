# Privacy Policy for Course Caddy

20 November 2025
## Introduction

Course Caddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect:** Your device's GPS location (when granted permission)
- **Why we collect it:** To automatically fetch weather conditions (temperature, elevation, humidity) for accurate club distance calculations
- **When we collect it:** Only when you use Pro features that auto-fill weather data
- **How it's stored:** Location data is used in real-time and NOT stored on our servers

### Golf Bag Data
- **What we collect:** Your club selections, distances, and baseline environmental variables
- **How it's stored:** All golf bag data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
- **Who can access it:** Only you - we do NOT have access to your golf bag data

### Purchase Information
- **What we collect:** In-app purchase receipts are processed by Apple
- **Who handles it:** Apple's App Store handles all payment processing
- **What we receive:** We only verify subscription status, not payment details

## Information We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- ❌ Payment card information
- ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Contacts or photos

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Location Data:** Used exclusively to fetch current weather conditions from Open-Meteo API for distance calculations
2. **Golf Bag Data:** Stored locally on your device to save your club configurations
3. **Subscription Status:** Verified through Apple's StoreKit to unlock Pro features

## Third-Party Services

### Open-Meteo Weather API
- **Purpose:** Fetch weather data for Pro users
- **Data Shared:** GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) or city name
- **Privacy Policy:** https://open-meteo.com/en/terms

### Apple StoreKit
- **Purpose:** Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **Privacy Policy:** https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Data Security

- All golf bag data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
- Location data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to weather services
- We do not maintain any servers that store user data
- No user accounts or registration required

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all data stored locally in the app (via My Golf Bag screen)
- **Delete:** Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- **Control Location:** Manage location permissions in iOS Settings → Course Caddy
- **Opt-Out:** Don't grant location permission to use the app without auto-weather features

## Children's Privacy

Course Caddy does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no age-restricted content.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.

## Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:
- **Email:** support@course-caddy.com
- **Website:** https://course-caddy.github.io/Course-Caddy-Website/

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Delete the Course Caddy app from your device
2. All locally stored golf bag data will be permanently removed
3. To cancel subscriptions, go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions

**Note:** This app does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
