# Privacy Policy for Course Caddy

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

## Introduction

Course Caddy LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What we collect:** Your device's GPS location (when granted permission)
- **Why we collect it:** To automatically fetch weather conditions (temperature, elevation, humidity, wind) for accurate club distance calculations
- **When we collect it:** Only when you use Pro features that auto-fill weather data
- **How it's stored:** Location data is used in real-time and NOT stored on our servers
- **How to control:** Manage location permissions in your device Settings → Course Caddy

### Golf Bag Data
- **What we collect:** Your club selections, distances, and baseline environmental variables
- **How it's stored:** All golf bag data is stored locally on your device using secure device storage (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS)
- **Who can access it:** Only you - we do NOT have access to your golf bag data
- **How to delete:** Uninstall the app to permanently remove all local data

### Purchase Information
- **What we collect:** In-app purchase receipts are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store
- **Who handles it:** Apple and Google handle all payment processing
- **What we receive:** We only verify subscription status, not payment details

### Website Contact Form
- **What we collect:** Name, email address, and message content when you submit the support form
- **Why we collect it:** To respond to your support requests, bug reports, and feature suggestions
- **How it's stored:** Form submissions are forwarded to our email (support@course-caddy.com) via FormSubmit.co
- **Retention:** We retain support emails as long as necessary to provide assistance
- **Third party:** FormSubmit.co processes form submissions (Privacy Policy: https://formsubmit.co/privacy)

## Information We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal identification information (except when you voluntarily provide it via support form)
- ❌ Payment card information (handled by Apple/Google)
- ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data
- ❌ Device identifiers for advertising
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Contacts or photos
- ❌ Social media information

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Location Data:** Used exclusively to fetch current weather conditions from Open-Meteo API for distance calculations
2. **Golf Bag Data:** Stored locally on your device to save your club configurations
3. **Subscription Status:** Verified through Apple StoreKit or Google Play Billing to unlock Pro features
4. **Support Requests:** Email and message content used to provide customer support

## Third-Party Services

### Open-Meteo Weather API
- **Purpose:** Fetch weather data for Pro users
- **Data Shared:** GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) or city name
- **Privacy Policy:** https://open-meteo.com/en/terms

### OpenStreetMap Nominatim (Geocoding)
- **Purpose:** Convert GPS coordinates to city names for weather lookup
- **Data Shared:** GPS coordinates
- **Privacy Policy:** https://operations.osmfoundation.org/policies/nominatim/

### Apple StoreKit & Google Play Billing
- **Purpose:** Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **Apple Privacy Policy:** https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Google Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

### FormSubmit.co
- **Purpose:** Process website contact form submissions
- **Privacy Policy:** https://formsubmit.co/privacy

## Data Security

- All golf bag data is stored locally on your device using platform-secure storage
- Location data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to weather services
- We do not maintain any servers that store user data
- No user accounts or registration required
- Website forms use HTTPS encryption

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all data stored locally in the app (via Golf Bag screen)
- **Delete:** Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- **Control Location:** Manage location permissions in device Settings → Course Caddy
- **Opt-Out:** Don't grant location permission to use the app without auto-weather features
- **Contact Us:** Request deletion of support emails by contacting support@course-caddy.com

### California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (Note: We do NOT sell personal information)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact support@course-caddy.com

### European Users (GDPR)
If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:
- Right of access to your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

## Children's Privacy

Course Caddy does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or 16 in Europe). The app is rated 4+ and contains no age-restricted content. If we learn we have collected information from a child under the applicable age, we will delete it promptly.

## International Data Transfers

Our service providers (Open-Meteo, FormSubmit) may process data outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## Data Retention

- **Golf Bag Data:** Stored locally on your device until you delete the app
- **Location Data:** Not retained - used in real-time only
- **Support Emails:** Retained as long as necessary to provide assistance
- **Weather Cache:** Temporarily cached locally for performance, cleared regularly
